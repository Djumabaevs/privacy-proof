ProofSnap Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Privacy First

Our Commitment

ProofSnap is designed with privacy at its core. Your photos are analyzed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. We never see, store, or transmit your actual images. Only a cryptographic hash (a unique fingerprint) and metadata you choose to include are sent to our server for blockchain anchoring.

Your photos never leave your phone. Only the SHA-256 hash of your image is transmitted to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Data Processed On-Device Only (Never Sent to Us)

  • Your photos — analyzed locally using Apple Vision framework for object detection, text recognition, and authenticity scoring
  • Photo library access — only when you explicitly select a photo from your gallery

Data Sent to Our Server

Data Purpose
Image hash (SHA-256) Unique fingerprint for blockchain anchoring
Timestamp Record when the proof was created
Device model identifier Part of proof metadata
GPS coordinates (if permitted) Location proof metadata
AI analysis results Objects detected, text recognized, authenticity score

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your actual photos or images
  • Your name, email, or any personal identity information
  • Contacts, messages, or browsing history
  • Analytics or advertising identifiers
  • We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs

2. Device Permissions

Camera

Required to take photos for proof creation. Camera data is processed on-device only. You can deny camera access and still use the app by selecting photos from your gallery.

Location (Optional)

Used to attach GPS coordinates to your proof, strengthening its evidentiary value. Location is requested only when in use and is never tracked in the background. You can deny location access and the app will function normally without it.

Photo Library

Used only when you explicitly choose to select a photo from your gallery instead of taking a new one. We do not scan or access your photo library in the background.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Blockchain anchoring — Your image hash and metadata are batched into a Merkle Tree and the root is anchored on the Polygon blockchain, creating an immutable proof of existence
  • Proof verification — Anyone with a proof ID or QR code can verify that a proof exists and when it was created
  • No other use — We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond proof creation and verification

4. Data Storage and Security

On Your Device

Proof records and thumbnail images are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data remains on your device and is protected by iOS device encryption.

On Our Server

Image hashes and metadata are stored in an encrypted SQLite database on our server. The server is hosted on Hetzner infrastructure with SSL/TLS encryption for all communications. No actual images are ever stored on our server.

On the Blockchain

Merkle roots (derived from batches of image hashes) are permanently recorded on the Polygon blockchain. Blockchain records are immutable and public by nature. No personally identifiable information is stored on-chain — only cryptographic hashes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. The only external system that receives data is the Polygon blockchain, which receives only Merkle root hashes (not individual image hashes or metadata).

Proof verification pages are publicly accessible by design — this is the core feature that allows anyone to verify a proof's authenticity.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local data: You can delete all local proof records at any time from Settings > Clear History
  • Server data: Proof records are retained on our server indefinitely to support verification. Contact us to request deletion of your server-side records
  • Blockchain data: Data anchored on the blockchain is immutable and cannot be deleted. This is by design — immutability is what gives the proof its value

7. Children's Privacy

ProofSnap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect personal identity information from any users, this risk is minimal.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny any device permission (camera, location, photo library) and still use core app functionality
  • Delete all local proof data at any time
  • Request deletion of your server-side proof records
  • Request information about what data we have associated with your proofs

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
